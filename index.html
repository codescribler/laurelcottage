Our Services

Comprehensive dental care for the whole family, from routine check-ups to advanced treatments

Routine Check-ups & X-Rays

Regular examinations to maintain your oral health and catch any issues early.

Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged teeth and replace missing ones with natural-looking restorations.

Hygienist Treatment

Professional cleaning and expert advice on maintaining healthy teeth and gums.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions that look, feel and function like natural teeth.

Dentures

Custom-fitted full and partial dentures for a comfortable, confident smile.

Tooth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments to brighten your smile safely and effectively.

Root Canal Therapy

Save infected teeth with gentle, effective root canal treatment.

Nervous Patients Programme

Gentle, understanding care for anxious patients in a calm, supportive environment.

White Fillings

Mercury-free, tooth-coloured fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.
